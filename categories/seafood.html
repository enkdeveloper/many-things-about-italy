Seafood Recipes

Linguine alle Vongole

Linguine pasta with thinly sliced clams (vongole) and garlic.

Ingredients:
  • Linguine pasta
  • Clams
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • White wine
  • Parsley
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
Instructions:

1. Cook linguine pasta according to package instructions.
2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
3. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and sauté until fragrant.
4. Add cleaned clams and white wine to the pan. Cover and cook until the clams open.
5. Toss cooked linguine with the clam sauce.
6. Garnish with chopped parsley, salt, and black pepper.
7. Serve hot.

Cioppino

Fish soup made with various seafood (shrimp, squid, mussels) and tomato sauce.

Ingredients:
  • Shrimp
  • Squid
  • Mussels
  • Fish fillets
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • White wine
  • Fish stock
  • Olive oil
  • Parsley
  • Bay leaf
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
Instructions:

1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat.
2. Add minced garlic and chopped onion, and sauté until translucent.
3. Add diced tomatoes, fish stock, white wine, bay leaf, and salt. Simmer for 20 minutes.
4. Add seafood (shrimp, squid, mussels, fish fillets) to the pot and cook until seafood is cooked through.
5. Garnish with chopped parsley, salt, and black pepper.
6. Serve hot with crusty bread.

Grilled Salmon

Grilled salmon fillet served with lemon slices and fresh herbs.

Ingredients:
  • Salmon fillets
  • Lemon
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Fresh herbs (such as dill, parsley, or thyme)
Instructions:

1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
2. Brush salmon fillets with olive oil and season with salt and black pepper.
3. Grill salmon fillets for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until cooked through.
4. Serve hot with lemon slices and garnish with fresh herbs.

Shrimp Scampi

Delicious seafood dish made with garlic, butter, white wine, and shrimp.

Ingredients:
  • Shrimp
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • White wine
  • Lemon juice
  • Parsley
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
Instructions:

1. In a large pan, melt butter over medium heat.
2. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and sauté until fragrant.
3. Add shrimp to the pan and cook until they turn pink.
4. Pour in white wine and lemon juice, and simmer until the sauce thickens slightly.
5. Season with salt and black pepper.
6. Garnish with chopped parsley.
7. Serve hot over pasta or with crusty bread.

Seafood Risotto

Creamy risotto made with shrimp, squid, mussels, and other seafood.

Ingredients:
  • Arborio rice
  • Shrimp
  • Squid
  • Mussels
  • Fish stock
  • White wine
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Parsley
  • Lemon zest
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
Instructions:

1. In a saucepan, heat the fish stock and keep it warm.
2. In another large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
3. Add minced garlic and chopped onion, and sauté until translucent.
4. Add Arborio rice and toast for a few minutes.
5. Pour in white wine and stir until absorbed.
6. Add a ladleful of warm fish stock to the rice and stir continuously until absorbed. Repeat this process until the rice is creamy and cooked al dente.
7. Stir in cooked shrimp, squid, and mussels.
8. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese, butter, chopped parsley, and lemon zest.
9. Season with salt and black pepper.
10. Serve hot.

Clam Chowder

American-style clam soup made with clams, potatoes, onion, and cream.

Ingredients:
  • Clams
  • Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Bacon
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Fish or vegetable stock
  • Heavy cream
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Parsley
Instructions:

1. In a large pot, cook chopped bacon until crispy. Remove bacon from the pot and set aside.
2. In the same pot, melt butter over medium heat. Add chopped onion and diced potatoes, and sauté until softened.
3. Stir in flour and cook for a few minutes to make a roux.
4. Gradually add fish or vegetable stock, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
5. Add clams (with their juices) to the pot and simmer until clams are cooked.
6. Stir in heavy cream and cooked bacon.
7. Season with salt, black pepper, and chopped parsley.
8. Serve hot with crusty bread.

Grilled Octopus

Grilled octopus served with olive oil, lemon, and pepper.

Ingredients:
  • Octopus
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon
  • Garlic
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
Instructions:

1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
2. Clean the octopus and remove the beak.
3. Cook the octopus in boiling water for about 30-45 minutes, or until tender.
4. Drain the octopus and pat dry.
5. Brush with olive oil and season with salt, black pepper, minced garlic, and red pepper flakes.
6. Grill the octopus for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until charred.
7. Serve hot with lemon wedges.

Fried Calamari

Deep-fried calamari rings served with lemon slices and marinara sauce.

Ingredients:
  • Calamari
  • Flour
  • Cornmeal
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon slices
  • Marinara sauce
Instructions:

1. Clean the calamari and slice into rings.
2. In a bowl, mix flour, cornmeal, salt, and black pepper.
3. Heat olive oil in a deep fryer or large pot.
4. Dredge the calamari rings in the flour mixture and shake off excess flour.
5. Fry the calamari rings in batches until golden brown and crispy.
6. Drain on paper towels and season with salt.
7. Serve hot with lemon slices and marinara sauce.

Seafood Paella

Spanish rice dish made with shrimp, mussels, squid, and other seafood.

Ingredients:
  • Short-grain rice
  • Shrimp
  • Mussels
  • Squid
  • Chicken or vegetable broth
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Bell pepper
  • Frozen peas
  • Saffron threads
  • Paprika
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon wedges
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
Instructions:

1. In a paella pan or large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
2. Add chopped onion, minced garlic, and diced bell pepper, and sauté until softened.
3. Stir in diced tomatoes, saffron threads, and paprika. Cook until tomatoes are softened.
4. Add short-grain rice to the pan and stir to coat with the vegetable mixture.
5. Pour in chicken or vegetable broth and bring to a simmer.
6. Arrange shrimp, mussels, and squid over the rice.
7. Cover and cook until the rice is tender and the seafood is cooked through.
8. Garnish with frozen peas and lemon wedges.
9. Serve hot.

Tuna Tartare

Raw, chopped tuna mixed with onions, parsley, and olive oil.

Ingredients:
  • Sushi-grade tuna
  • Red onion
  • Parsley
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
Instructions:

1. Chop sushi-grade tuna into small pieces and place in a bowl.
2. Finely dice red onion and chop parsley, then add to the bowl with the tuna.
3. Drizzle with lemon juice and olive oil.
4. Season with salt and black pepper.
5. Gently toss to combine.
6. Serve immediately as a tartare appetizer.
