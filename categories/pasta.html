Pasta Recipes

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Spaghetti cooked in olive oil with garlic and red pepper flakes.

Ingredients:
  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Salt
Instructions:

1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
2. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
3. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes to the pan. Cook until garlic is fragrant but not browned.
4. Add cooked spaghetti to the pan and toss to coat with the garlic-infused oil.
5. Season with salt to taste.
6. Serve hot.

Penne all'Arrabbiata

Penne pasta served with a spicy tomato sauce.

Ingredients:
  • Penne pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Fresh basil (optional)
Instructions:

1. Cook penne pasta according to package instructions.
2. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
3. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes to the pan. Cook until garlic is fragrant but not browned.
4. Add diced tomatoes to the pan and cook until they break down and form a sauce.
5. Season with salt to taste.
6. Toss cooked penne pasta with the spicy tomato sauce.
7. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired.
8. Serve hot.

Lasagna

Layered Italian lasagna made with thinly sliced meat, cheese, and béchamel sauce.

Ingredients:
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Nutmeg
Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. In a pan, cook ground beef with diced onion and minced garlic until browned.
3. Add tomato sauce to the pan and simmer for 10 minutes.
4. In a separate saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in flour to create a roux.
5. Gradually whisk in milk until smooth. Cook until thickened. Season with salt, black pepper, and nutmeg.
6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with beaten eggs.
7. Spread a layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
8. Arrange lasagna noodles over the sauce.
9. Spread a layer of ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by a layer of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
10. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of cheese on top.
11. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
12. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until bubbly and golden.
13. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Ravioli al Burro e Salvia

Ravioli served in a butter and fresh sage sauce.

Ingredients:
  • Ravioli
  • Butter
  • Fresh sage leaves
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
Instructions:

1. Cook ravioli according to package instructions.
2. In a pan, melt butter over medium heat.
3. Add fresh sage leaves to the pan and cook until crispy.
4. Drain cooked ravioli and add to the pan.
5. Toss ravioli in the butter and sage sauce until coated.
6. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
7. Serve hot with grated Parmesan cheese on top.

Fettuccine Alfredo

Fettuccine pasta served in a creamy and cheesy Alfredo sauce.

Ingredients:
  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Heavy cream
  • Butter
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Nutmeg
Instructions:

1. Cook fettuccine pasta according to package instructions.
2. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.
3. Pour in heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
4. Gradually stir in grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
5. Season with salt, black pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg.
6. Drain cooked fettuccine and add to the sauce.
7. Toss until the pasta is well coated in the Alfredo sauce.
8. Serve hot.

Orecchiette with Broccoli Rabe

Orecchiette pasta with finely chopped broccoli rabe and sautéed garlic.

Ingredients:
  • Orecchiette pasta
  • Broccoli rabe
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
Instructions:

1. Cook orecchiette pasta according to package instructions.
2. Blanch broccoli rabe in boiling water for 2 minutes, then drain and chop finely.
3. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
4. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes to the pan. Cook until garlic is fragrant but not browned.
5. Add chopped broccoli rabe to the pan and sauté for a few minutes.
6. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
7. Toss cooked orecchiette pasta with the broccoli rabe mixture.
8. Serve hot with grated Parmesan cheese on top if desired.

Spaghetti Carbonara

Classic Italian dish made with eggs, pancetta, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper.

Ingredients:
  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Eggs
  • Pancetta
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
Instructions:

1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
2. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, black pepper, and a pinch of salt.
3. In a pan, cook diced pancetta until crispy.
4. Drain cooked spaghetti and add to the pan with pancetta.
5. Pour the egg mixture over the spaghetti and toss until the eggs are cooked and the pasta is coated in the sauce.
6. Serve hot with additional Parmesan cheese and black pepper on top.

Pappardelle al Cinghiale

Pappardelle pasta with wild boar meat and tomato sauce.

Ingredients:
  • Pappardelle pasta
  • Wild boar meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • Garlic
  • Red wine
  • Beef broth
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)
Instructions:

1. Cook pappardelle pasta according to package instructions.
2. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
3. Sauté diced onion, carrot, and celery until softened.
4. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
5. Add wild boar meat to the pan and cook until browned.
6. Deglaze the pan with red wine and cook until reduced by half.
7. Stir in tomato sauce and beef broth. Simmer for 30 minutes.
8. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
9. Toss cooked pappardelle pasta with the wild boar ragu.
10. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Gnocchi Sorrentina

Baked gnocchi served with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and basil.

Ingredients:
  • Gnocchi
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Cook gnocchi according to package instructions.
3. In a baking dish, spread a layer of tomato sauce.
4. Arrange cooked gnocchi over the sauce.
5. Top with mozzarella cheese slices.
6. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and black pepper.
7. Bake for 20 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
8. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.
