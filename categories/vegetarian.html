Vegetarian Recipes

Pesto Pasta

Delicious pasta served with a pesto sauce made with fresh basil, pine nuts, parmesan cheese, garlic, and olive oil.

Ingredients:
  • Pasta
  • Fresh basil
  • Pine nuts
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
Instructions:

1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
2. In a food processor, blend fresh basil, pine nuts, parmesan cheese, garlic, and olive oil until smooth.
3. Toss the cooked pasta with the pesto sauce.
4. Season with salt and black pepper.
5. Serve hot.

Eggplant Parmesan

Layered dish made with fried eggplant slices, tomato sauce, and mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients:
  • Eggplant
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Bread crumbs
  • Egg
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Slice the eggplant into rounds and dip in beaten egg, then coat with bread crumbs.
3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat and fry the eggplant slices until golden brown.
4. In a baking dish, layer fried eggplant slices with tomato sauce and mozzarella cheese.
5. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of mozzarella cheese and parmesan cheese on top.
6. Bake for about 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
7. Serve hot.

Caprese Sandwich

Delicious sandwich made with mozzarella cheese, tomato slices, and fresh basil.

Ingredients:
  • Bread
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
Instructions:

1. Slice the bread and toast if desired.
2. Layer sliced mozzarella cheese, tomato slices, and fresh basil leaves on one slice of bread.
3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
4. Season with salt and black pepper.
5. Top with another slice of bread.
6. Serve immediately.

Vegetable Lasagna

Layered lasagna made with vegetables (spinach, zucchini, carrots) and béchamel sauce.

Ingredients:
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Spinach
  • Zucchini
  • Carrot
  • Tomato sauce
  • Béchamel sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
3. In a pan, sauté chopped spinach, sliced zucchini, and grated carrots until tender.
4. In a baking dish, layer lasagna noodles, tomato sauce, sautéed vegetables, béchamel sauce, and mozzarella cheese.
5. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of mozzarella cheese and parmesan cheese on top.
6. Bake for about 30-35 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
7. Serve hot.

Stuffed Bell Peppers

Bell peppers stuffed with rice, vegetables, and spices, then baked until tender.

Ingredients:
  • Bell peppers
  • Rice
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Corn
  • Black beans
  • Cumin
  • Chili powder
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes.
3. In a pan, sauté chopped onion and minced garlic in olive oil until softened.
4. Add cooked rice, diced tomato, corn, black beans, cumin, chili powder, salt, and black pepper.
5. Stir to combine and cook for a few minutes.
6. Stuff the bell peppers with the rice mixture.
7. Place the stuffed peppers in a baking dish and cover with foil.
8. Bake for about 25-30 minutes, or until the peppers are tender.
9. Serve hot.

Vegetarian Pizza

Pizza topped with vegetables (bell peppers, mushrooms, olives) and mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients:
  • Pizza dough
  • Bell peppers
  • Mushrooms
  • Olives
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
Instructions:

1. Roll out the pizza dough on a baking sheet.
2. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
3. Top with sliced bell peppers, mushrooms, and olives.
4. Sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese.
5. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and black pepper.
6. Bake in a preheated oven at 425°F (220°C) for about 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
7. Serve hot.

Quinoa Salad

Healthy salad made with red and white quinoa, fresh vegetables, herbs, and olive oil.

Ingredients:
  • Red quinoa
  • White quinoa
  • Tomato
  • Cucumber
  • Red onion
  • Parsley
  • Mint
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
Instructions:

1. Cook red and white quinoa according to package instructions and let cool.
2. In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, diced tomato, chopped cucumber, thinly sliced red onion, chopped parsley, and chopped mint.
3. Drizzle with lemon juice and olive oil.
4. Season with salt and black pepper.
5. Toss to combine.
6. Serve chilled.

Vegetable Stir Fry

Quick and healthy stir fry made with mixed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, bell peppers) and soy sauce.

Ingredients:
  • Broccoli
  • Carrots
  • Bell peppers
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
Instructions:

1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat.
2. Add chopped onion, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Stir fry for a minute.
3. Add sliced carrots, broccoli florets, and sliced bell peppers. Stir fry for a few minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
4. Pour in soy sauce and sesame oil.
5. Season with salt and black pepper.
6. Stir to combine.
7. Serve hot over cooked rice or noodles.
