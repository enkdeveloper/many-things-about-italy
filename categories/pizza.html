Pizza Recipes

Margherita Pizza

Classic Italian pizza topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil.

Ingredients:
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Pizza dough
Instructions:

1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature setting.
2. Roll out the pizza dough into a circle.
3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a border around the edges.
4. Tear fresh mozzarella cheese into pieces and scatter over the sauce.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
6. Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves.
7. Slice and serve hot.

Calzone

Folded pizza filled with cheese, meat, and vegetables.

Ingredients:
  • Pizza dough
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ham
  • Mushrooms
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions
Instructions:

1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature setting.
2. Roll out the pizza dough into a circle.
3. Spread tomato sauce over half of the dough, leaving a border around the edges.
4. Top with mozzarella cheese, ham, mushrooms, bell peppers, and onions.
5. Fold the other half of the dough over the toppings and seal the edges.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
7. Slice and serve hot.
